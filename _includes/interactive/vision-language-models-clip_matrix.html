Temperature τ scales logits before softmax. Lower τ = sharper distribution (correct pairs dominate). Higher τ = flatter distribution (all pairs get similar probability). The symmetric cross-entropy loss averages image-to-text and text-to-image directions. Matrix values change with τ because they show softmax probabilities, not raw similarities.
